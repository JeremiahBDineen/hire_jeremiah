About Me

My name is Jeremiah Dineen and I'm a full-stack data analyst with 5+ years of analytics experience across finance, healthcare, and the telecommunications industry. I build end-to-end data solutions — ingesting and transforming messy data with Python and SQL, modeling outcomes in R or scikit-learn, and delivering dashboards in Tableau. My work includes forecasting mortgage delinquency, preparing clinical trial datasets, automating reporting pipelines at scale, and applying statistical modeling — including regression techniques — to generate grounded inference and robust, methodologically sound business insights. I hold an M.S. in Biostatistics and specialize in GLMs, causal inference, and KPI-driven analysis.
